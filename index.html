SOCIAL MEDIA BOON OR BANE?

What social media ?

Websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking.

Why is it called social media?

This ability to communicate directly and immediately with the source of news is a major change in the way information gets handled. Because of its direct, one-to-many-and-back-again method, it's called “social.”
